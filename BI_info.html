What does each role do?

The Data Analyst's role is to provide actionable insights to the business through dashboards, reports and data extracts. This requires them to work with the business users, and the Business Analyst, to understand what the business requirments are. They then manipulate the data into an understandable format and extract key insights before reporting back to the business users.

A medium sized BI team typically has four or more Data Analysts.
