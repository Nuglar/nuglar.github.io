Engagement KPIs

Problem

Company EG is a member based not-for-profit organisation. One of their primary KPIs is how engaged their potential and paying members are with their content. However, engagement is difficult to put numbers to, let alone track. In preparation for their monthly meetings, the executive of Company EG us to check statistics on how members opened their newsletter, and how many likes and comments their social media posts received. Checking any more regularly than that was considered too much effort, and didn't seem to bring any benefit to the business.

Solution

daRta was brought in to build a solution to help Company EG track and report on their engagement KPIs. Each night, Company EG's new business intellignece platform automatically connects to all their outreach platforms to collect engagement data. This includes: Twitter, Instagram and Facebook for social media likes, shares and comments; Google Analytics for website interactions; and Mailchimp for newsletter engagement levels. A Sentiment analysis AI categorises social media mentions, and the total reach of a post, including the networks of the people sharing it, is calcualted. All this information is then fed into an interactive report that is automatically sent to the executives inbox the day before their weekly meeting. The report can also be accessed on-demand with near-real-time information for retrospective analysis of marketing and engagement strategies.

Result

The executive of Company EG now have visability of their engagement KPIs throughout the month. With more regular access to engagement data, discussions on the effectiness of recent engagement and marketing stategies are now part of the executives weekly meetings. As a result, Company EG have since seen an increase in both number of members and member engagement as their content becomes more refined and targeted.
