About Us

Who are we?

Our vision is for small businesses to have access to the same tools and skills that big businesses rely on so heavily.

For this to work, we've had to redefine what it means to be a business intelligence consultancy.

At daRta, we are dedicated to providing the level of support you can expect from an IT service provider, but with the passion of someone who works for your business.

We're here because we are passionate about making a difference, and we want to help you make a difference too.




What makes us different?

Working for small business has its unique challenges for business intelligence firms. Small businesses require affordable solutions that start small and scale with them. They need solutions that don't complicate their business, but simplify it.

At daRta, we specialise in providing these solutions for small business. We don't want to change the way you work, we want to reduce the amount of work you do while enhancing your decision making capabilities. We do this by forging ongoing partnerships, where you only pay for what you need, when you need it.

What's with the R?

At daRta, we believe that good business intelligence is an aRt. The job of a data analyst is much like that of a storyteller; they take something as abstract as data, find the story worth telling, and turn it into meaningful information.

The R shaped like a 2 is also a reference to the statistical term R2, which is used to express how well a statistical model explains data and thus how informative it is. R also just so happens to be the founder's favourite programming language.

Who's in our team?

The Business Analyst is the role you will interact with most. Their job is to work with the client to understand the business requirements and coodinate the rest of the team. Think of them as the glue that holds the team together.

The Data Engineer is in charge of getting data from source systems into a structure that can be easily accessed by the Data Scientist and Data Analyst. Think of them as plumber - they get water from a source reservoir into your home, so you don't have to make the trip with a bucket yourself.

The Data Analyst takes data prepared by the Data Engineer and transforms it into something that is accessible and meaningful to the business user. Think of them as a translator - they will translate raw data into information you can action in meaningful ways.

The Data Scientist uses advanced analytical techniques such as statistical analysis, machine learning, deep learning and artificial intelligence to solve more complex business problems. Think of them as you would a scientist - they are there to push the boundaries of knowledge.

The Cyber Security specialist's role is to actively and passively ensure that your data is secure within the platform. Think of them as a bouncer standing at the gates, and only your name is at the door!
