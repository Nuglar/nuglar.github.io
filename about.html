What's with the R?

We believe that good business intelligence is an art. The job of a data analyst is much like that of a storyteller; they take something as abstract as data, find the story worth telling, and turn it into meaningful information using a medium appropriate for the target audience. It is for the reason that the R is placed in between the A and T of data.

If you want to get really nerdy, the shape of the R can also be read as a 2 – a reference to the common statistical term “R2”. R also just happens to be the founders favourite programming langauage. The R just works!
