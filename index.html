Our Services

We are Australia's business intelligence firm for small business and startups

At daRta, we understand that a small business's most valuable asset is its people. Thus, the success of a business is determined by how its people spend their time.

Our mission is to empower you to spend less time in excel, and more time taking informed action based on reliable information.

About Us



Business Intelligence is no longer the domain of big business

Business intelligence - the art of transforming data into actionable information - can seem inaccessible to small businesses due to the time required to do it oneself, the costs associated with hiring people with data skills, and the price of software.

Yet every business, regardless of size, relies on information to make decisions and take informed action.

At daRta, we are proud to be providing affordable services that are custom fit to your needs, so you can spend your time where it really matters - running the business.
